Our Story

Founded in 2020, Petals & Blooms started with a single rose and a big dream. Our mission is to transform ordinary days into extraordinary memories through the language of flowers. We believe that every bouquet is a message of love, hope, and celebration.

Why We Are Different:

  • 🌸 Direct Sourcing: We get our flowers daily from local farms to ensure maximum freshness.
  • 🎨 Expert Florists: Our team has over 15 years of artistic floral design experience.
  • 🌍 Eco-Friendly: We care for nature as much as we love flowers, using 100% biodegradable packaging.
